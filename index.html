My Story
From finance backroom to centre stage.

I started in finance doing what most finance professionals are trained to do: work hard, be accurate, stay in the background, let the numbers speak. For years, I believed that if my work was strong enough, it would automatically be noticed.

It did not work that way. While working with founders, CEOs, and boards I realized the gap between competence and visibility. I started consciously packaging my thinking and presenting myself as a strategic thinker, not just a numbers guy.

Today, I work with CFOs and senior finance leaders who are at a similar inflection point. They are competent, experienced, and trusted with the numbers, but they feel their influence is capped. If no one can see how you think, they cannot trust you with bigger decisions.
